IRA PLANT SHILAJIT

IRA Plant Shilajit is natural and 100% organic. IRA Plant Shilajit increase water retention or the water-holding capacity of soils, helps retain moisture and support plant growth during dry periods by reducing water stress.

Available:

250 ml 500 ml 1 liter 10 liters
Description

IRA PLANT SHILAJIT contains more than 70 naturally occurring minerals, amino acids, sugars, peptides, nucleic acids, phytochemical compounds, vitamins, and fragments of plant DNA. It can be added to the soil, used as a foliar spray, or applied directly to the roots. When used as a soil amendment, it can help to improve nutrient uptake and increase the overall health of the plant. It is a novel antimicrobial molecule that is reported to have antibacterial and antifungal properties.

Additional Information
  • IRA Plant Shilajit is natural and 100% organic.
  • IRA Plant Shilajit increase water retention or the water-holding capacity of soils, helps retain moisture and support plant growth during dry periods by reducing water stress.
  • Promotes quicker seed germination and faster root and shoot growth.
  • Helps buffer soil pH, maintaining a more stable and favorable pH range for plant growth.
  • Able to chelate (bind) essential nutrients like minerals and trace elements, making them more soluble and mobile in the soil.
  • Can complex with nutrients at the root surface, facilitating their uptake by plant roots, improves nutrient absorption efficiency and reduces the risk of nutrient leaching.
