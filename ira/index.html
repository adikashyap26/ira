Applications

Agriculture
(Organic Farming)

Agriculture is the art and science of cultivating the soil, growing crops and raising livestock.

Plant Bio-tech

Plant biotechnology (PBT) encompasses a multitude of scientific tools and techniques for screening and genetic manipulation of plants to develop beneficial or useful plant/plant products.

Industrial

Industrial agriculture is a form of modern farming that refers to the industrialized production of crops and animals and animal products like eggs or milk.

 

Custom Production

Custom production follows a made-to-order model. Workers customize the product during production so that it meets the needs of the customer who ordered it.

Tissue Culture

Tissue culture, a method of biological research in which fragments of tissue from an animal or plant are transferred to an artificial environment in which they can continue to survive and function.

Environment

Using organic media for agricultural purposes can have both positive and potential negative impacts on the environment, depending on various factors.
