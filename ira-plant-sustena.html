IRA PLANT SUSTENA

IRA PLANT SUSTENA is designed specially for urban planters to experience a happy and sustainable organic gardening. Also to help soil to benefit the chemical free organic supplements and produce beautiful flowers and vegetables under controlled conditions of home.

Description
IRA PLANT SUSTENA is designed specially for urban planters to experience a happy and sustainable organic gardening. Also to help soil to benefit the chemical free organic supplements and produce beautiful flowers and vegetables under controlled conditions of home.
Additional Information

How to use:

  • Introduce one pouch per pot/plant, once a month

Benefits:

  • Improves and optimizes nutrient uptake, Increases aeration and rejuvenates soil.
  • Less water consumption, optimizes soil pH
  • Faster seed Germination
Application
Vegetable & Fruit Production, Terrace Gardening, Balcony Gardening, Backyard Gardening, Seed Treatment
