IRA PLANT PEARLS

Humic acids are organic compounds that are formed through the decomposition of organic matter, such as dead plant material, in the soil. Earthworms play a crucial role in the production and enrichment of humic acids in the soil through their activities.

Store in dry place, protect it from heat, frost and direct sunlight. Keep out of reach from children and pets.

Available:

250 g 500 g 1 kg
Description

It chelates nutrient compounds, thereby optimizing the nutrient supply of plants. Ira Plant Pearls helps to increase the yield and lead to healthier growth of green grass, ornamentals, agricultural crops (fruits and vegetables) turf and meadows, and woods. It helps to reduce the use of fertilizers and pesticides. Furthermore, water holding capacity of soils is increased considerably, which means that the use of water can be reduced substantially.

Additional Information

IRA Plant PEARLS, is natural and 100% organic.

  • Darken the color of the soil and thus help absorption of the solar energy.
  • Increased water holding capacity of soil.
  • Increased aeration of soil.
  • Neutralize both acid and alkaline soils; regulate the pH-value of soils.
  • Improve and optimize the uptake of nutrients and water by plants.
  • Stimulate plant enzymes and increase their production.
  • Act as an organic catalyst in many biological processes.
  • Act as natural chelator for metal ions under alkaline conditions and promote their uptake by the roots.
  • IRA Plant supplements biologically stimulate the plant and the activities of micro-organisms.
  • The ecological benefits of humic acids are diverse and present profitable and effective solutions for environmental problems and the preservation of the environment.
